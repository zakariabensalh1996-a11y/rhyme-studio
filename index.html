Welcome! Let's make a nursery rhyme video.

Everything you do here is saved automatically in your browser. No account, no cost.

🎶 Step 1 — Make the song

Write your rhyme, then turn it into music + singing with a free tool.

Tip: keep lines short and repeat the chorus — toddlers love repetition.
🎵 Open Suno (free song) 🎼 Open Udio

🧸 Step 2 — Design your characters

Describe each character once, generate a picture, and reuse it everywhere so they stay consistent.

🎨 Leonardo.ai (free images) 🖼️ Bing Image Creator ✨ MS Designer

🎬 Step 3 — Build your scenes (shot list)

Break the song into short shots. For each one: make an image, then animate it into a 3–5s clip.

🎥 Kling AI (free video) 🌊 Hailuo / MiniMax ⚡ Pika

👄 Step 4 — Voices & lip-sync (optional)

Make a character's mouth move to the singing, or add spoken lines.

🗣️ Hedra (talking faces) 🎙️ ElevenLabs (free voice)

✂️ Step 5 — Edit it all together

Drop your clips on the song's timeline, cut to the beat, add captions, export.

✂️ CapCut (free editor) 🎞️ DaVinci Resolve
